About us

Started by two history teachers with passions for baking, A Time for Sweets opened in 2016. Previous history students have helped develop ideas for the various confectionaries produced here.

We offer:

  • Washington's Cherry Pie
  • Lexington and Concord Grape Jam Tart
  • Let Them Eat Chocolate Cake
  • Paul Revere's Midnight Rice Crispy
  • Johnny Appleseed Fritter
  • Eric the Red Velvet Cupcake
  • Many more to come!
